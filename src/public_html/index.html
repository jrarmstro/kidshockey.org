FAQ

  • The Kids Christian Hockey League is an introductory hockey program for children ages 5 — 15
  • General schedule runs from early September until the end of February
  • One hour a week combining skills training and games
  • Learn to Skate is a 30 minute program
  • Full hockey equipment is required for all ages
  • We have equipment for sale at a very affordable price
  • We don't cancel ice time, even if your child has a snow day
